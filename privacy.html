Klypr Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy
Last updated April 2026  ·  On-device. No tracking. No BS.
📌

What is Klypr?

Klypr is a Chrome extension that adds timestamped notes on YouTube watch pages — so your ideas stay pinned to exact moments in any video.

💾

Data we store

Notes, timestamps, optional titles, tags, and UI preferences (like your theme) are saved using chrome.storage.local directly in your browser. This data lives on your device only and is tied to the YouTube video you're watching. We do not operate a Klypr account or sync server.

🚫

What we do not do

  • ✕
    Sell your data to anyone, ever
  • ✕
    Use your data for advertising or profiling
  • ✕
    Receive your stored notes on our servers
  • ✕
    Use your data for credit decisions or unrelated purposes
🔑

Permissions

The extension runs on https://www.youtube.com/* to display the sidebar and timeline pins on watch pages. This matches the host permission in the extension manifest — nothing more.

🔗

Third-party services

Typography may be loaded from Google Fonts. That request is subject to Google's own terms and privacy policies.
