Klypr Privacy Policy

Klypr is a Chrome extension that adds timestamped notes on YouTube watch pages.

Data we store

Notes, timestamps, optional titles, tags, and UI preferences (such as theme) are saved with chrome.storage.local in your browser. This data is stored on your device only. We do not operate a sync server for this data.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not receive your stored notes on our servers.

Permissions

The extension runs on https://www.youtube.com/* to show the sidebar and pins on watch pages.

Third-party services

Typography may load from Google Fonts, subject to Google's terms.
